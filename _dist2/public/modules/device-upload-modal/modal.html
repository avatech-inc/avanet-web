SP1 USB Data Upload

SP1 USB upload requires the Google Chrome browser

Download Google Chrome
1. Power on your SP1
2. Plug your SP1 into your computer
Does your SP1 screen show "USB CONNECTED"?
After clicking "Select SP1", click on your SP1 in the left-hand menu, then click "Upload"
After clicking "Select SP1", click on your SP1 under "Computer" in the folder list, then click "OK"
