Welcome to the AvaTech testing program! We hope this testing program will be a rewarding experience for you and we are immensely grateful for your support and feedback. With your help, we believe we have a unique opportunity to bring a new and exciting technology to the market that can help all of us be safer in the backcountry. Please keep in mind this is not a finished product, but a work in progress prototype, and that your input will be invaluable to building this product to the highest standard of the industry.

This web platform allows you to upload field data from your AvaTech device, create manual snow profiles, and submit your field tests to AvaTech for data analysis. We've worked hard to make this process as easy and streamlined as possible, and we encourage you to share your feedback with us. Thank you for your support!

- Team AvaTech

Instructions

Before you begin: Thoroughly read the Testing Procedures Guide. To ensure accurate data from your device, please familiarize yourself with the field testing procedures outlined in this guide.
After collecting data in the field, follow these simple steps to create and submit field tests:

1Sync your device
Uploads your latest AvaTech device profiles to the cloud.
2Create manual profile
Our snowpit editor allows painless entry of your manual snow profiles.
3Input your field test report
Per your field notes, associate your uploaded AvaTech device profiles with your manual snow profile, and submit your report to AvaTech.
